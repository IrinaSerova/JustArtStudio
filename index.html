About studio

Leon Okun’s studio is a unique environment where one can learn traditional European academic methods of painting and drawing. The studio is composed of two instructors: Leon Okunand Varvara Agabekova.

Both Leon and Varvara are graduate of St. Petersburg Academy of Fine Arts in Russia. Leon and Varvara have different approaches to painting and teaching, which allows students to try different approaches to painting.

Both styles represent the 260 year old tradition of St. Petersburg Academy of Fine Arts. Leon and Varvara are both working artists, which serves as a source of example and inspiration to the students. Leon and Varvara are the only representatives of traditional European academic painting in southern California.

What we do and how we do it:

We work from life, portrait, still life, and plein air. We start from simple assignments and advance to multi figured compositions.

Plein Air Classes

Oil on Canvas, this class will be outdoors! $25 per hour, for schedule please refer to the calendar. Plein Air is a French term that means “in the open air” referring to painting outdoors. Plein air became especially popular with impressionists, who rediscovered landscape painting and perfected color theory. Like impressions we will leave our dark studios with artificial light and brown color pallets and paint outdoors! To discover color harmonies of sunrise, afternoon, ocean scenes, estuaries, mountains, and San Diego urban life. Experience the difference in light between summer, fall and winter. Plein air class is a great way to learn composition, color harmony, drawing as well as get to know San Diego from an artist point of view!

Portrait Class

We will work from life model, $25 per hour for a class more than three students, $50 per hour for individual lessons. Class available up on request, for dates please refer to the schedule. Portrait Painting from life model is a very challenging class, but it is difficult to find something more exiting! Trying to catch the likeness of the sitter, we will explore composition, drawing and color harmonies. We will talk about how to start working from big planes and generalization to smaller form, down to the smallest details. We will have live model pose for us at least six hours to complete our study..
